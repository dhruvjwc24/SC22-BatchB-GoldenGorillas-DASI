What is DASI ?

DASI is a Machine Learning Model for detecting abnormalities in skeletal X-Rays.


Goals

  • Helping radiologists with their jobs
  • Speeding up the X-Ray / diagnostic process
  • Helping educators and medical students by utilizing our product as an education aid

    • The Process

    DASI takes input from x-ray images and classes them to either fracture or metal-rod fracture* , making the process faster for Radiologist to determine where the skeletal abnormality is.



    Accuracy & Error

    Our model can accurately detect various types of fractures in bones. It's not accurate enough to be relied on, but it can be used as an aid in diagnostics.

    The timeline

    Our Journey and Challenges
    6/27/22 - 7/15/22

    • Week 1

      Data Collection

      We collected data of bone fractures and metal-rod fractures* using webscraping tools and datasets found online.
      Data was scarce, so we only managed to get around 400 images. We managed to triple our dataset to over 1200 imaged through Data Augumentation.

    • Week 2

      Annotating and Training

      With the data that we have annotated, we put the annotated data to their right class, making it supervised learning. After we finished annotating all the data, we Augment it to have a better model.

    • Week 3

      Website Development

      Created a website to show our model to the world, using html, css, and flask. Learning about the front-end and back-end.

    • AI Camp

      With AI Camp we learn a lot about AI, Machine learning, and deep learning. It help us improve our knowledge based around technology, how to collect, annotate, and train data, building websites with front-end and back-end. AI Camp helps students with communication and teamwork skills.
